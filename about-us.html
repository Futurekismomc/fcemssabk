History Of the School

The School was established in September, 2002/2003 session by proprietor, the Provost Dr. J.O. Akinmoyewa. Academic activities commenced on 2nd October 2002 with thirty six (36) students and twelve (12) in J.S.S. 2

The good leadership quality has lead the school to experience different levels of achievement. In order to re-strategies the school for education excellence he school moved to her permanent site on 1st November, 2007.

The school is enriched with highly qualified, diligent and committed members of staff. There are facilities such as well-equipped laboratories for practical based subject, good transportation system, medical personnel, sporting equipment, library to mention, but a few. The school has turned out high number of students in WAEC/NECO over the years with commedable performances which has earned them admission in reputable institutions in Nigeria and Diaspora

The school currently runs a boarding house, where students are given parental care; indeed it is a home away from home and second home indeed. This school adopts a management committee, this had led to change in power at the expiration of a tenure

The school as at now is headed by Mrs S.I.C Iwuagwu a chief lecturer in the school of Language, FCE Abeokuta and the principal is Mr. David Adaralegbe.The school now as about 1050 students
